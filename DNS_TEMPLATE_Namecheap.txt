Namecheap DNS Template for Vercel + Zoho (replace PLACEHOLDERs after adding domain in Vercel/Zoho)

1) Website (Vercel)
- CNAME Record
  Host: www
  Value: CNAME_TARGET_FROM_VERCEL  (example: cname.vercel-dns.com)
  TTL: Automatic

- URL Redirect Record (301)
  Host: @
  Value: https://www.retiredtoken.com
  TTL: Automatic

(Optional) If Vercel shows A records instead of CNAME for apex:
- A Record
  Host: @
  Value: 76.76.21.21
  TTL: Automatic

2) Email (Zoho Mail)
- MX Record (Priority 10)
  Host: @
  Value: mx.zoho.com
- MX Record (Priority 20)
  Host: @
  Value: mx2.zoho.com
- MX Record (Priority 50)
  Host: @
  Value: mx3.zoho.com

- SPF (TXT)
  Host: @
  Value: v=spf1 include:zoho.com ~all

- DKIM (TXT)  [selector example: zoho]
  Host: zoho._domainkey
  Value: PASTE_DKIM_VALUE_FROM_ZOHO

- DMARC (TXT) (optional but recommended)
  Host: _dmarc
  Value: v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; rua=mailto:info@retiredtoken.com; ruf=mailto:info@retiredtoken.com; fo=1

After saving records in Namecheap, return to Vercel (Domains) and Zoho (Mail Admin) and click Verify.
